Client Service Manager

As Client Service Manager, your primary responsibility is to provide our clients - institutional investors incl. hedge funds, private equity firms and venture capital – with rapid access to relevant industry and professional specialists who help our clients understand critical issues in the context of their investment decisions across European markets. Our clients count among the most sophisticated investors in the world and expect the highest standard of professional service.
As a result, you must possess a personal strive for excellence and a strong dedication to client service in order to meet or exceed our clients’ expectations.

Your responsibilities include:

  • Managing the client account relationship (phone, email and sometimes in person) on a day to day basis with view to capturing, understanding and validating our clients’ research needs (Delivery Brief)
  • Owning and leading the delivery (search and communication) process with a strong sense of accountability to the client on multiple concurrent assignments. This specifically involves:
    • Making dynamic time and resource allocation decisions
    • Managing a project-specific team of Research Associates
    • Identifying, contacting (by email and phone) and winning over relevant candidates
    • Refining the Delivery Brief (with Recruitment Associates and possibly with the client) to ensure delivery of relevant individuals in response to any client request
    • Determining ultimate suitability in light of the Delivery Brief of any specialist candidates that have been recruited
  • Over time, developing a relationship of trust and understanding with the principal contacts at our clients
  • Contributing to the specification and refinement of internal tools and member acquisition campaigns
  • Keeping abreast of best practice with a view to ensuring the delivery process evolves yielding improved efficiency and quality

Requirements:

You are likely to have a background at a leading executive search, management consulting or industry polling company. In any case, dealing with demanding clients is second-nature to you and you take pride in the quality of your service. In addition, you should possess:

  • Bachelor or Master’s Degree from a leading university
  • 4 years’ minimum relevant work experience in a client facing delivery role in a high-level professional-services environment
  • Innate curiosity, interest and proven ability to appreciate the key aspects and dynamics of multiple industries and businesses
  • Ability to multi-task and work independently in a fast-paced environment
  • Ability to quickly adapt to changing priorities and willingness to regularly go “above and beyond”
  • Proficiency in web search and tools, MS Office and CRM applications
  • Entrepreneurial spirit and a “can do” attitude
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Engaging personality with a successful track record working with top-class clients and in a success driven team environment
  • Knowledge of a foreign language desirable

Compensation:

Remuneration is highly competitive and will reflect your contribution to our company’s overall success. As a young, fast-growing company we offer excellent career opportunities and you are expected to progress. Please send your online application including your CV, your salary expectations and a brief covering letter outlining why you think you are suitable for this role to careers@alphasights.com. Contact via email only. No agencies please.
