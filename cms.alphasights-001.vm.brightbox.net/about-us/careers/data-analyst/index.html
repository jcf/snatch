Data Analyst

As Data Analyst, you will be responsible for verifying the integrity and improving the density of data in the proprietary databases underlying our business system. In addition, you will be responsible for systematically collecting and analysing performance data providing company management with actionable intelligence to improve processes and decision-making.

Your responsibilities include:

  • Analysing the proprietary databases underlying our business system to ensure all data points are as accurate and complete as possible
  • Identifying and accessing novel sources of data and cross-referencing multiple databases with a view to maximising the number of relevant searchable contacts in our databases
  • Working with company management and various departments to define relevant performance metrics for all major processes
  • Systematically collecting and analysing the data and reporting to management

Requirements:

  • Bachelor or Master’s Degree from a leading university in statistics, mathematics, or related science
  • 2-3 years’ minimum relevant work experience in data mining and data analysis, e.g. in web campaign measurement
  • Excellent knowledge of and experience in working with complex relational databases
  • Exceptional analytical/problem resolution skills
  • Proficiency in statistical programs such SPSS, in addition to MS Excel/Access
  • The ability to present effectively verbally and in writing
  • The ability to multi-task and work independently in a fast-paced environment
  • Ability to quickly adapt to changing priorities

Compensation:

Remuneration is highly competitive and will reflect your contribution to our company’s overall success. As a young, fast-growing company we offer excellent career opportunities and you are expected to progress. Please send your online application including your CV, your salary expectations and a brief covering letter outlining why you think you are suitable for this role to careers@alphasights.com. Contact via email only. No agencies please
