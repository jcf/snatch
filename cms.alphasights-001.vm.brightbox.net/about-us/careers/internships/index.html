Internships / Summer Positions

AlphaSights offers undergraduate and postgraduate students with an interest in financial services, strategy consulting or executive search the opportunity to gain valuable work experience in a fast-paced professional work environment.

Positions are available in our core business (providing instant access to industry expertise in response to client needs) and in special project areas where you can make use of your areas of interest and proficiency (e.g., technology, editorial and translation, marketing, finance, legal and compliance).

Requirements:

  • Undergraduate or graduate degree at a leading university (completed or nearing completion)
  • Energetic personality with a “can do” attitude and organisational talent
  • Self-starter with the ability to work independently
  • Proficient in MS Office, Email and Internet applications
  • Foreign language skills desirable

Internships are available year-round. Opportunity to join us full-time after graduation is subject to performance. Please send your online application including your CV, your areas of particular interest and a brief covering letter outlining why you think you are suitable to careers@alphasights.com. Contact via email only. No agencies please.
