Recruiting Associate

As Recruiting Associate, your primary responsibility lies in identifying and successfully approaching potential members for our AlphaCircles. AlphaCircle Members are senior-level executives and industry professionals with a proven track record of achievement who can provide our investor clients with an early stage view on a company, technology or industry, through to conducting specific due diligence consultancy assignments and in some cases participating in a bid and possibly joining the management team after a successful transaction.

Your responsibilities include:

  • Identifying, contacting (by email and phone) and convincing suitable candidates of the benefits of membership.
  • Ad-hoc recruiting of specialists required in response to urgent client requests
  • Following the strict guidelines of our Admissions Policy, you will conduct systematic industry research to continuous update and grow our database of prospective members
  • Utilising web tools such as RSS feeds, Google Alerts etc. to keep yourself abreast of relevant industry and company developments
  • Liaising with both potential and actual members by email and phone and providing timely and courteous support
  • Helping conduct our member marketing initiatives and events incl. regular member surveys, newsletters, and industry-sector meetings

Requirements:

You are likely to have a background at a leading executive search firm or a leading global B2B conference company. In either case, dealing with senior decision-makers is second nature to you. You enjoy approaching, meeting and engaging with new people and you possess a natural talent for networking. In addition, you should possess:

  • University degree
  • 2 years’ minimum relevant work experience in a professional-services firm in executive search, consulting, conference management or similar
  • Ability to multi-task and work independently in a fast-paced environment
  • Ability to quickly adapt to changing priorities
  • Proficiency in web search and tools
  • Entrepreneurial spirit and a “can do” attitude
  • Excellent verbal and written communication skills
  • Engaging personality with a successful track record working in a team environment
  • Knowledge of a foreign language desirable

Compensation:

Remuneration is highly competitive and will reflect your contribution to our company’s overall success. As a young, fast-growing company we offer excellent career opportunities and you are expected to progress. Please send your online application including your CV, your salary expectations and a brief covering letter outlining why you think you are suitable for this role to careers@alphasights.com. Contact via email only. No agencies please.
