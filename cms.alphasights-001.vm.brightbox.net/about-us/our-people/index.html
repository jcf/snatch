Our people

AlphaSights was founded by serial entrepreneurs Max Cartellieri and Andrew Heath, who serve as the company’s Managing Directors. Headquartered in London, AlphaSights has assembled a group of talented and experienced professionals recruited from a wide variety of professional backgrounds.

Max Cartellieri

Prior to AlphaSights, Max Cartellieri co-founded and served as co-CEO of Ciao AG, Europe’s leading price comparison and product review service, acquired by Microsoft (MSFT) in 2008.

Over the period 1999-2006, Max built the company from a founding team of two into a profitable, fast-growing information-services business employing more than 300 employees with offices in Europe, North America, Asia and Australia.

Prior to co-founding Ciao, Max worked at McKinsey & Company and private-equity investors BC Partners.

Max holds a B.Sc. (1st class) in Economics as well as an M.Sc. in Finance from the London School of Economics, and an MBA from the Stanford University Graduate School of Business, where he was an Arjay Miller Scholar.
