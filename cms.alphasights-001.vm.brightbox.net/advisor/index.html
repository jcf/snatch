Advisors

AlphaSights Advisors are active professionals recognized within their industries and professions as thought leaders possessing deep expertise and pertinent frontline insights. In exchange for their willingness to share concise, accurate and actionable insights at short notice in consultations with our Clients, Advisors receive attractive hourly compensation and benefit from the opportunities that engaging with AlphaSightsâ€™ sophisticated client base can entail. Learn more about the benefits that participation entails.

Catering to the different availability and requirements of Advisors, two advisory programs are currently available:

Expert Advisor Program

Expert Advisors generally or have until recently been in full-time employment in either industry or research and share their specialist insights with Clients on an hourly basis in ad-hoc telephone consultations. Our Expert Advisors include industry executives, doctors, economists, engineers, lawyers, researchers and other professionals who deliver expertise and decision-making assistance to our Clients in the business and investment community.

Read about our Expert Advisor Program

Executive Advisor Program

Executive Advisors are senior-level professionals with a significant track record of achievement who are outside of current employment obligations and thus available to participate in longer-term, in-depth advisory opportunities.

Our Executive Advisors possess deep-sector knowledge gained in senior-level management roles or at partner-level in specialist consulting or advisory firms. Executive Advisors include serial entrepreneurs, recently exited buy-out managers, and senior professionals on sabbatical or parental leaves. Other recognized industry experts who meet our rigorous selection criteria are invited to apply as long as they can provide us with written confirmation from their employer that they are eligible to participate in in-depth advisory opportunities.
