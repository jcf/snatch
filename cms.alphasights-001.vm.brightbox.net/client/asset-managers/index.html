Investment Managers

Our clients in investment management use AlphaSights for a variety of ways in their quest for superior investment returns: to quickly familiarize themselves with new industries or companies in their research universe; to keep taps on critical business drivers; or to deepen their understanding of regulatory, technological or scientific developments.

How AlphaSights helps

Industry research

As investment managers initiate coverage of new industries, companies or investment themes, leapfrogging the learning curve through direct one-on-one consultations with experienced industry professionals is by far the most time-efficient way to get to grips with the specific dynamics, trends and key players in a given industry.

Investment monitoring

Investment managers wish to form their own views - independent of management forecasts or consensus opinion – of critical business drivers such as demand- or supply-side channel dynamics, customer satisfaction levels, or pricing trends. AlphaSights provides them with timely access to industry insiders’ individual or aggregated assessments of the dynamics and evolution of critical business drivers affecting target or portfolio companies.

Catalyst identification

Superior returns oftentimes result from the ability to identify catalysts challenging widely held beliefs or breaking previously stable trends. Whether rooted in regulatory, behavioural, technological or scientific developments, AlphaSights can provide investment managers with timely access to subject-matter experts who can alert or comment knowledgeably upon such potential catalysts.

How Clients benefit

Gain Superior Industry Understanding

By connecting ad-hoc and as-needed with a thoughtfully chosen array of experienced industry professionals, our clients gain access to expertise and insights otherwise inaccessible or unaffordable. Our proprietary data services help investment managers to stay attuned to the dynamics and evolution of critical business drivers affecting target or investee companies.

Save time

Investment managers operating across borders and industries do not have the time to identify, contact, qualify, schedule, negotiate and settle fees with a large number of potentially relevant industry professionals. Leveraging proprietary search methodologies and our unique relationships, AlphaSights is uniquely placed to source, select and vet an array of qualified industry professionals possessing precisely the expertise and insights our clients seek.

Maintain Confidentiality

We are acutely aware of the sensitive nature of our clients’ business. Our systems and in-built workflows are designed such that client confidentiality is maintained at all times prior to the actual contact with one or more chosen industry professionals taking place. In the case of survey and proprietary data services, client identity is never revealed.
