Professional Advisory Firms

Our professional advisory clients including strategy consultancies and M&A advisors use AlphaSights at various stages of their client service process from internal knowledge development through pitch preparation and client service.

How AlphaSights helps

Knowledge Development

The best professional advisors help shape the agenda of their clients rather than merely reacting to it. AlphaSights supports professional advisory firms in their quest for thought leadership through facilitating interviews with and surveys of industry experts that help identify and track industry, technological or behavioural developments long before they become received wisdom

Client Development

Prior to approaching prospective clients, professional advisory firms use AlphaSights to consult with industry experts whose familiarity with the prospective clients and their key decision-makers helps tailor pitches precisely to prospective clients’ needs.

Client Service

Professional advice is both most actionable and most credible when it draws on factual evidence and perceptive insight. Both can be sourced efficiently either one-on-one consultations with experienced industry professionals, or through surveys and panels that aggregate industry insiders’ assessments of relevant market variables.

How Clients benefit

Save Time

Professional advisors’ time is best spent on developing and serving their clients, backed up by the best research support there is. Only few global firms possess the resources required to identify, contact, qualify, schedule, train and settle fees with a potentially large number of relevant industry professionals themselves. In most instances, AlphaSights is better placed to accomplish these steps efficiently and minimize both costs and time demands.

Enhance client service

The advisory firms we serve possess a profound understanding of the structural features and major drivers of the industries in which their clients operate. Still every new engagement requires its own assessment of the dynamics and evolution of critical business drivers affecting clients and their industries. AlphaSights helps these client firms to efficiently source the factual foundations for their advice, helping them advance the credibility and impact of their recommendations.

Maintain Confidentiality

AlphaSights is aware of the sensitive nature of our clients’ business. Our systems and in-built workflows are designed such that client confidentiality is maintained at all times prior to the actual contact with one or more chosen industry professionals taking place. In the case of survey and proprietary data services, client identity is never revealed.
