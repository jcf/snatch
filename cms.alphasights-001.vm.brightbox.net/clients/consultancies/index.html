Professional Advisory Firms

Our professional advisory clients including strategy consultancies and M&A advisors use AlphaSights at various stages of their client service process from internal knowledge development through pitch preparation and client service.

How AlphaSights helps

Knowledge Development

The best professional advisors help shape the agenda of their clients rather than merely reacting to it. AlphaSights supports professional advisory firms in their quest for thought leadership through expert interviews, surveys and industry expert panels that help identify and track industry, technological or behavioural developments long before they become received wisdom.

Business Development

Prior to approaching prospective clients, professional advisory firms use AlphaSights to consult with industry experts whose familiarity with the prospective clients and their key decision-makers enables uniquely tailored pitches addressing prospects’ precise needs.

Client Service

Professional advice is both most actionable and most credible when it draws on factual evidence and perceptive insight. Both can be sourced efficiently either one-on-one consultations with experienced industry professionals, or through surveys and panels that aggregate industry insiders’ assessments of relevant market variables. AlphaSights helps professional advisory firms to efficiently source the factual foundations for their advice, helping them advance the credibility and impact of their recommendations.

How Clients benefit

Enhance client service

The advisory firms we serve possess a profound understanding of the structural features and major drivers of the industries in which their clients operate. AlphaSights’ role is to provide timely and precision-targeted access to industry insiders and thought leaders who can individually or in aggregation help assess the dynamics and evolution of critical business drivers such as customer demand, pricing, supply-cost trends, or technological, regulatory and environmental developments.

Save Time

Professional advisory firms do not have the time to identify, contact, qualify, schedule, negotiate and settle fees with a vast number of potentially relevant industry professionals. Leveraging proprietary search methodologies and our strategically positioned Industry Partners, AlphaSights is uniquely placed to accomplish these steps efficiently and minimize time demands placed on busy professionals and their support staff.

Maintain Confidentiality

AlphaSights is aware of the sensitive nature of our clients’ business. Our systems and in-built workflows are designed such that client confidentiality is maintained at all times prior to the actual contact with one or more chosen industry professionals taking place. In the case of survey and proprietary data services, client identity is never revealed.
