Private Equity & Venture Capital

Our clients in private equity and venture capital work with us throughout the investment process from early-stage deal sourcing through due diligence, portfolio monitoring and realisation.

How AlphaSights helps

Sourcing

AlphaSights connects investment professionals at client firms with senior-level industry executives who can provide an early-stage view on acquisition opportunities, elaborate on industry trends, or help assess key industry drivers. Depending on client needs, initial phone-based consultations may be followed up by face-to-face meetings or lead to longer-term advisory relationships with chosen executives. Highly targeted phone consultations can typically be arranged within 1-3 days.

Due Diligence

AlphaSights supports investment professionals to confirm or refute their investment thesis by helping them to systematically probe into demand and supply dynamics, competitive forces, or technological or regulatory factors impacting target companies. Dedicated research teams advise our clients on the most appropriate and efficient method of obtaining the required insights. Our research teams then source, select and vet an array of qualified industry professionals to determine those possessing precisely the expertise and insights our clients need.

Portfolio Monitoring

As Board Directors, our clients possess a profound understanding of the structural features and major drivers of the industries in which their portfolio companies operate. AlphaSights provides them with ad-hoc or continuous access to industry insiders’ individual or aggregated assessments of the dynamics and evolution of critical business drivers such as customer demand, pricing, supply-cost trends, or technological, regulatory and environmental developments.

How Clients benefit

Gain Superior Industry Understanding

By connecting directly with a thoughtfully chosen array of experienced industry professionals, our clients gain access to expertise and insights otherwise inaccessible. In addition, our proprietary data services enable clients to keep taps on relevant industry trends and developments before they become mainstream news.

Save Time

Principal investors operating across borders and industries do not have the time to identify, contact, qualify, schedule, negotiate and settle fees with a vast number of potentially relevant industry professionals. Leveraging proprietary search methodologies and our strategically positioned Industry Partners, AlphaSights is uniquely placed to accomplish these steps efficiently and minimize time demands placed on busy investment professionals and their support staff.

Maintain Confidentiality

We are acutely aware of the sensitive nature of our clients’ business. Our systems and in-built workflows are designed such that client confidentiality is maintained at all times prior to the actual contact with one or more chosen industry professionals taking place. In the case of survey and proprietary data services, client identity is never revealed.
