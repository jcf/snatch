One-on-One meetings

Private one-on-one meetings with AlphaSights Advisors can be arranged to take place at clients’ offices or around industry events or conferences. Clients benefit from in-depth sessions with Advisors who can provide detailed insight and perspective on the chosen topic.

Based on each client’s specific need, a dedicated AlphaSights research team will:

  • Identify the most qualified and appropriate Advisors
  • Pre-screen their ability to address the client’s specific questions
  • Deliver a summary of their professional qualifications
  • Obtain their consent compliance and confidentiality requirements
  • Agree their remuneration
  • Schedule the meeting
